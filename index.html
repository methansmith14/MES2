Horror Hunter Handbook

Those under Villains are aware and intelligent. They can plan, trick, communicate, etc. Monsters are those that only care about self sustenance and food and their next meal.They can be intelligent but only want food. Unknown are those above human comprehension. The middle between villain and monster. The Danger Rating is a scale from most dangerous to least. This guide should give you an idea of these evildoers and how to take them down.
